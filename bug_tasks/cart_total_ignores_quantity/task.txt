Fix the bug in the shopping cart total calculation.

The cart total should equal the sum of each line item's product price multiplied by its quantity.

Requirements:
- A cart with one item of quantity 1 should return that product's price.
- A cart with one item of quantity greater than 1 should multiply by quantity.
- A cart with multiple items should sum all line totals.
- Invalid product price should throw IllegalArgumentException.
- Invalid quantity should throw IllegalArgumentException.
- Do not change public method signatures.
